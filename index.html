About us

Quartz – is an international investment company based in London and Moscow.

We invest in disruptive technology companies that strive to become leaders in critical markets.

With unprecedented access to major corporate partners in Europe and the United States, we open doors for the companies we invest in and help create conditions to accelerate business.

In addition to private equity, we also invest in new and exceptional venture capital funds to empower them to support great entrepreneurs around the world.
